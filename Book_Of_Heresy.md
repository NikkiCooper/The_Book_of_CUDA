# 🚫 The Book of Heresy 🚫

---

## Heresy 1: The Macro of Temptation 🪤
Beware the macro that promiseth convenience,  
For it hideth the serpent of undefined behavior.

---

## Heresy 2: The Unsafe Pointer 🧨
They who casteth recklessly shall surely segfault.

---

## Heresy 3: The Infinite Loop 🌀
The loop that knoweth no end shall consume all cycles  
And bring darkness upon the render.

---

## Heresy 4: The Memory Leak 🪦
The leak that is not freed shall grow until the system falleth.

---

## Heresy 5: The Deprecated Function 🐍
The function marked deprecated is the whisper of the old serpent;  
Use it not, lest thou be cursed.
